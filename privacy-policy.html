Privacy Policy

We are serious about protecting your privacy.

At Daypack, we value and respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy demonstrates our firm commitment to your privacy. We appreciate your use of our application and encourage you to read this privacy policy to understand how we collect, use and process your information.

Information Collected

We collect information that you share with us when using Daypack.

Profile Information: When you sign up for Daypack certain information is required such as your full name, email address and company name. Daypack does not use or disclose personal information for purposes other than those for which it was collected, except with the consent of the individual or as required by law. Daypack retains this information only as long as the profile is active.

Requesting Information: When you request a demo, you are required to enter your name, company name, email address and telephone number. Daypack does not use or disclose personal information for purposes other than those for which it was collected, except with the consent of the individual or as required by law.

We collect information automatically when using Daypack.
